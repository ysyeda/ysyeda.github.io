Hello! I’m Moaaz Sidat, a designer & developer living in Waterloo, ON. I’m pursuing a Bachelors in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo with a heightened interest towards Human Computer Interaction.


I currently work as a Product Designer at MappedIn.


I love obsessing over typographic details, enjoy talking and thinking about product design, prototyping and front-end technologies. I enjoy building products and crafting experiences surrounding them.


I spend some of my time trying to learn something new. These days my focus lies mostly towards Objective-C and Motion Design. I’m a cat-lover and an occasional photographer. I enjoy hand-lettering & illustrations, and will sometimes explore those two worlds of design purely for fun.


I’m currently looking for small scale front-end development or interface design projects. Let’s chat if you have something in mind.
